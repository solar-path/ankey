# Getting Started with Solo

Welcome to Solo's multi-tenant business management platform! This guide will help you get up and running quickly.

## Overview

Solo provides a comprehensive business management solution with:

- **Multi-tenant architecture** for workspace isolation
- **Role-based access control** (RBAC) for security
- **Compliance tools** for SOX/SOC requirements
- **Audit logging** for complete activity tracking

## Quick Start

### 1. Create Your Workspace

1. Go to the [homepage](/) and click "Sign Up"
2. Fill out the workspace registration form:
   - **Workspace name**: This becomes your subdomain
   - **Full name**: Your name as the administrator
   - **Email**: Your business email address
   - **Password**: Strong password with uppercase, lowercase, and numbers

3. Click "Create Workspace" to submit

### 2. Email Verification

After registration:

- Check your email for a verification link
- Click the link to verify your account
- You'll receive another email when your workspace is ready

### 3. Access Your Workspace

Your workspace will be available at:

```
https://[your-workspace-name].localhost:3000
```

### 4. Initial Setup

When you first log in:

1. **Review your profile** - Update your information if needed
2. **Set up two-factor authentication** - Recommended for security
3. **Invite your first users** - Add team members to your workspace
4. **Configure roles** - Set up appropriate permissions

## Core Concepts

### Workspaces (Tenants)

- Each workspace is completely isolated
- Has its own database and subdomain
- Workspace administrators control user access
- Billing is per workspace

### Users and Permissions

- **Workspace Admin**: Full control over the workspace
- **Standard Users**: Access based on assigned roles
- **Role-based Access**: Granular permissions system
- **Delegation of Authority**: Temporary permission delegation

### Audit and Compliance

- **Complete Audit Trail**: Every action is logged
- **Safe Delete**: Data marked as deleted but retained for compliance
- **SOX/SOC Compliance**: Built-in compliance reporting
- **Data Retention**: Configurable retention policies

## Common Tasks

### Inviting Users

1. Go to **Users** > **Invite User**
2. Enter email and full name
3. Select appropriate roles
4. Send invitation

### Creating Roles

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Roles**
2. Click **Create Role**
3. Set permissions for each resource
4. Assign to users as needed

### Viewing Audit Logs

1. Go to **Compliance** > **Audit Logs**
2. Filter by user, action, or date range
3. Export reports for compliance review

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't access workspace**

- Check that you're using the correct subdomain
- Verify your email and password
- Clear browser cache and cookies

**Invitation emails not received**

- Check spam/junk folders
- Verify email address is correct
- Contact workspace administrator

**Permission denied errors**

- Contact your workspace administrator
- Submit a "Let Me In" request if appropriate
- Review your assigned roles

# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: {new Date().toLocaleDateString()}_

At Solo, llc, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our multi-tenant business management platform.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create a workspace or user account, we collect:

- **Workspace Details**: Name, subdomain, billing information
- **User Information**: Full name, email address, password (encrypted)
- **Business Information**: Company details, user roles, permissions

### Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use our services:

- **Audit Logs**: All user actions for compliance and security
- **System Logs**: Technical data for performance monitoring
- **Analytics**: Aggregated usage patterns to improve our services

### Technical Information

- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, IP address
- **Performance Data**: Page load times, error reports, feature usage
- **Security Data**: Login attempts, access patterns, security events

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### Service Provision

- **Account Management**: Creating and managing your workspace
- **Access Control**: Implementing role-based permissions (RBAC)
- **Compliance**: Maintaining audit trails for SOX/SOC requirements
- **Support**: Providing customer service and technical assistance

### Security and Compliance

- **Audit Logging**: Recording all user actions for compliance
- **Security Monitoring**: Detecting and preventing unauthorized access
- **Data Protection**: Implementing multi-tenant isolation
- **Compliance Reporting**: Generating required regulatory reports

### Service Improvement

- **Analytics**: Understanding how features are used
- **Performance Optimization**: Improving system performance
- **Feature Development**: Building new capabilities based on usage patterns

## 3. Data Architecture and Isolation

### Multi-Tenant Security

- **Database Isolation**: Each tenant has a separate PostgreSQL database
- **Data Segregation**: Complete logical and physical data separation
- **Access Controls**: Tenant-specific authentication and authorization
- **Network Isolation**: Subdomain-based tenant routing

### Data Storage

- **Primary Database**: PostgreSQL with per-tenant databases
- **Audit Storage**: Centralized logging with tenant identification
- **Backup Systems**: Encrypted backups with tenant-level recovery
- **Geographic Storage**: Data stored in secure, compliant data centers

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do Not Sell Your Data

Solo does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### Limited Sharing Scenarios

We may share your information only in these specific cases:

**Service Providers**

- Cloud infrastructure providers (with data processing agreements)
- Security monitoring services (for threat detection)
- Compliance auditing firms (for regulatory requirements)

**Legal Requirements**

- Court orders or legal process
- Government investigations (with appropriate legal basis)
- Protection of Solo's rights and property
- Prevention of fraud or security threats

**Business Transfers**

- In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
- With appropriate data protection guarantees
- After notifying affected customers

## 5. Data Security Measures

### Organizational Measures

- **Employee Training**: Regular security and privacy training
- **Access Policies**: Strict need-to-know access principles
- **Incident Response**: Documented procedures for security incidents
- **Compliance Monitoring**: Regular audits and compliance assessments

### Audit and Compliance

- **SOX Compliance**: Controls for financial data accuracy
- **SOC Reports**: Regular third-party security assessments
- **Audit Logging**: Immutable logs of all data access and changes
- **Data Retention**: Compliant data retention and deletion policies

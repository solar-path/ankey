# Cookie Policy

_Last updated: {new Date().toLocaleDateString()}_

This Cookie Policy explains how Solo, llc ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our multi-tenant business management platform.

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better, more secure experience by remembering your preferences, maintaining your session, and understanding how you interact with our services.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our platform to function properly and cannot be disabled:

**Authentication Cookies**

- `auth_session`: Maintains your login session
- `csrf_token`: Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks
- `tenant_context`: Identifies your workspace/tenant
- `user_preferences`: Stores your UI preferences and settings

**Security Cookies**

- `security_token`: Additional security verification
- `mfa_state`: Multi-factor authentication session state
- `rate_limit`: Prevents abuse and ensures fair usage
- `audit_session`: Links actions to audit log entries

**Functional Cookies**

- `theme_preference`: Dark/light mode setting
- `language`: Preferred language setting
- `timezone`: User timezone for date/time display
- `sidebar_state`: Navigation sidebar expanded/collapsed state

### Analytics and Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how our platform is used and improve its performance:

**Usage Analytics**

- `analytics_session`: Tracks user journey through the platform
- `feature_usage`: Records which features are being used
- `performance_metrics`: Measures page load times and responsiveness
- `error_tracking`: Helps identify and resolve technical issues

**A/B Testing**

- `experiment_groups`: Assigns users to feature testing groups
- `variant_tracking`: Tracks performance of different UI versions
- `feedback_eligibility`: Determines when to show feedback prompts

### Third-Party Cookies

We use limited third-party services that may set their own cookies:

**Infrastructure Services**

- **Content Delivery Network (CDN)**: For faster asset delivery
- **Load Balancer**: For distributing traffic efficiently
- **Monitoring Services**: For uptime and performance monitoring

**Payment Processing**

- **Stripe**: For secure payment processing (only on billing pages)
- **PayPal**: If PayPal payment option is selected
- **Tax Calculation**: For accurate tax computation by jurisdiction

## Cookie Consent and Management

### Consent Framework

When you first visit our platform, we'll ask for your consent for non-essential cookies:

- **Essential Cookies**: Always enabled (required for basic functionality)
- **Analytics Cookies**: Optional (help us improve the platform)
- **Marketing Cookies**: Optional (not currently used but reserved for future)
- **Preference Cookies**: Optional (enhance your experience)

### Managing Your Cookies

**Through Our Platform**

- Access cookie preferences through your user settings
- Toggle different cookie categories on/off
- View detailed information about each cookie
- Clear stored cookies and reset preferences

**Through Your Browser**
Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings:

- **Google Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
- **Mozilla Firefox**: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
- **Microsoft Edge**: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Cookies

**Browser Cookie Controls**

- Block all cookies (may break website functionality)
- Block third-party cookies only
- Delete existing cookies
- Set cookies to expire when browser closes
- Receive notifications before cookies are set

### Mobile Cookie Management

- **iOS Safari**: Settings > Safari > Block All Cookies
- **Android Chrome**: Chrome app > Settings > Privacy > Cookies
- **Mobile Apps**: Cookie settings available in app preferences

## Specific Cookie Details

### Session Cookies

**Purpose**: Maintain your login session and application state
**Duration**: Until browser session ends
**Data Stored**: Encrypted session identifier, user ID, tenant context
**Essential**: Yes - required for platform functionality

### Preference Cookies

**Purpose**: Remember your UI preferences and settings
**Duration**: 1 year
**Data Stored**: Theme, language, timezone, layout preferences
**Essential**: No - enhances user experience

### Analytics Cookies

**Purpose**: Track usage patterns and platform performance
**Duration**: 2 years
**Data Stored**: Anonymized usage data, feature interactions
**Essential**: No - helps improve platform

### Security Cookies

**Purpose**: Protect against security threats and fraud
**Duration**: 24 hours to 30 days depending on type
**Data Stored**: Security tokens, rate limiting counters
**Essential**: Yes - required for security

## Multi-Tenant Cookie Isolation

### Tenant Separation

Our multi-tenant architecture ensures cookie isolation between workspaces:

- **Subdomain Isolation**: Each tenant uses separate subdomain cookies
- **Data Segregation**: Cookies cannot access data from other tenants
- **Session Boundaries**: Login sessions are tenant-specific
- **Cross-Tenant Prevention**: Technical measures prevent data leakage

### Workspace Switching

When switching between workspaces (if you have access to multiple):

- Previous workspace cookies remain isolated
- New session cookies are created for the new workspace
- No data is shared between workspace sessions
- Each workspace maintains separate preferences
